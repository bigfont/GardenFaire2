Beautifully situated campsites in an old growth forest connecting to the 'off leash' Mouat Park Trails, just a 10minute walk to all the amenities in downtown Ganges.
   All the walk-in tent sites and the camper/RV sites have fire rings. The water throughout the campground is good for drinking and there are water and electric hook-ups in the camper/RV sites.
   We have a wonderful campground hostess and we welcome both children and camping-friendly dogs.
RATES per night:
Walk-in tent sites for 1 tent / 2 people... $22
Extra persons... $8 each    Children under 14 stay free
RV sites... $30 (15 Amp power and water hookup)
Prices incl. tax
